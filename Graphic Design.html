Graphic Design

Poster Design

GBDA 101

February 2024

This diptych presents a parks and recreation advertising narrative, inviting audiences to explore their local parks for peace and connection with nature. The right poster, titled "Find your peace outdoors," emphasizes escaping city stress to find tranquility, using park benches and serene backgrounds to evoke calmness. The left poster, with bold text "Explore nature today," encourages immediate action. Both posters include QR codes for easy access to more information, evoking a sense of adventure and discovery, and urging interaction with the natural world.

Magazine Design

GBDA 101

April 2024

The theme of the magazine is nature and growth, which refers to me in the field of design, going from a beginner to a skilled creator. Not only did I have to familiarize myself with tools in Adobe software, but I also had to understand the design principles, product composition and much more. Standing on the threshold of this course, I clearly recognize that I am a beginner who lacks expertise in this field, but I am passionate and interested in this career. Adobe's suite is a collection of possibilities, each of the tools helps me to create and refine the product to achieve creativity.

Trifold Brochure Design

High School Project

January 2023

This is a tri-fold brochure travel schedule brief designed to demonstrate my graphic design and typography skills. By organizing the text and visual elements, I have designed a simple, clear and visually appealing tri-fold for travelers to quickly understand the schedule, highlights of the location and detailed transportation information. The whole design style is fresh and modern, and the color scheme is tightly integrated with the content structure to help readers get useful information in a visually appealing atmosphere.
